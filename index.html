About Me

For me, editing is about finding the heartbeat of a story. I get obsessed with that perfect cut, that moment the music swells just right, or the piece of dialogue that changes everything.

My real passion is weaving together sound, picture, and pacing to create a specific feeling. I love the challenge of jumping between worlds—from the gritty atmosphere of a cinematic character study to the explosive energy of a gaming trailer or the professional polish of a live sports broadcast.

If you have a story that needs to feel powerful, emotional, and unforgettable, I have the skills and the dedication to help you bring it to life.

Let's make something incredible together.
